GCU Men's Volleyball Program History

The GCU Men's Volleyball program was founded in 2009. The team played its first game on February 10, 2008, but was officially established in 2009.

Elimination of the Program

Grand Canyon University (GCU) cut its men's volleyball program in April 2025 to "better support" their other 20+ athletic programs, according to the university. This decision was made despite the team's success. The university also mentioned the evolving college athletics landscape. Their plans to join the Mountain West Conference played a role in the cut as they don't sponsor men's volleyball.
